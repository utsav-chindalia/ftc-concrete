Our Journey

This group was formed by visionay "Sri Fateh Chand Chindalia" and it started the journey 40 years back as a logistic company.Its was started by the name FREIGHT TRANSPORT CORPORATION and gradually from goods carrier it became specialised carrier and it also registered itself as sa bank approved transporter.

From the experinced logistic company it started spreading the wings in the field of infrastructure and Ready Mix concrete(RMC).

The company is very aspiring & having a vision to expand RMC buisiness throught out India.
